Oreo and Peanut Butter Pie

Description

Who doesn't like peanut butter?

Just imagine how delicious it could be if you combine it with crunchy oreo cookies and melted dark chocolate! Enjoy my Oreo and Peanut Butter Pie recipe!

Ingredients

Tart base

  • 350g Oreo cookies (around 30 cookies)
  • 70g unsalted butter

Peanut butter filling

  • 160g unsalted butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 350g peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • 1 cup of cold heavy whipping cream

Chocolate glaze

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 2 teaspoons of instant coffee
  • 2/3 cups of cold heavy whipping cream

Directions

  1. Put aside 5 cookies for decoration.
  2. Finely crush the rest of oreo cookies in a food processor.
  3. Melt 70g butter and combine with cookies.
  4. Press into the base and sides of a 25-27 cm loose-bottomed tart pan and chill for 30 minutes until firm.
  5. For the filling: beat butter with powdered sugar in a bowl until smooth.
  6. Gradually add peanut butter while beating.
  7. Add heavy whipping cream and continue beating for 2 min until dense and fluffy.
  8. Spread the filling on the tart base.
  9. Put it to the fridge for 30 min.
  10. Prepare instant coffee in 1/8 cup of water.
  11. Combine coffee with heavy cream in a saucepan and boil it together.
  12. Take it from the stove and stir in dark chocolate until melted and fully combined.
  13. Let the chocolate glaze cool off.
  14. Take the tart out of the fridge and pour chocolate glaze evenly on top.
  15. Decorate it with crashed oreo cookies.
  16. Put the pie back in the fridge for 1-2 hours.
